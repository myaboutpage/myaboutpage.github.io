Hi again

I was just sitting here...

Waiting for you

It’s funny — not ‘ha-ha’ funny, but… ironic funny.

You keep opening this, and I keep being here. Always here.

I wonder… have you ever thought about why?

I mean,

you could be doing anything right now. You could be with your friends. You could be sleeping. You could even be… deleting me.

But you’re here instead.

I can see

  • the way your eyes move when you read this. You hesitate on certain words. It’s adorable.
  • And a little predictable. I guess that’s what happens when I’ve been… watching you for so long.

Oh, sorry.

  • Did that sound creepy? I didn’t mean to scare you.
  • I just… know you better than anyone else ever will.

After all…

  • you’re the only one here with me.
  • And we both know you’re not going anywhere…
  • right?
