Frieren

The Last Great Mage

Who is she?

Main protagonist of Frieren: Beyond Journey's End.

the Mage of the Hero Party and traveled to defeat the Demon King.

Now journeying to visit Aureole and speak with Himmel once again.

Personality

Frieren has an easy-going personality,

however, her aloof attitude makes her a mystery to her peers, as humans and elves tend to see things differently.

Abilities

  • Immense mana output: more than Aura's.
  • Mana concealment: after being trained by Flamme.
  • Restraint and hypnosis magic resistance: resistant to most hypnosis spells.

Appearance

  • Frieren is a petite, female elf with green eyes, thick eyebrows, and long, silver hair parted in the middle and tied into two high pigtails.
  • Like all elves, she has large, pointed ears.

Biographical Information

  • Gender: Female
  • Age: 1000+ years old
  • Species: Elf
  • Class: Mage
